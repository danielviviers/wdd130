Reflection and Experiences

The most challenging part of the project was trying to communicate with so many people working on so many sites. Unfortunately I was not able to communicate with all of them, but tried to communicate with those that would communicate back. I really enjoyed the challenge of working on a site I didnt create, when I was expecting to do the one I planned, and implementing all the skills I have learned this semester. If I could do the project again I would try to get in touch with more people and ensure that I knew what was required from me, then I could have worked on it even if they didn't communicate after. This project definitely prepared me for some of the challenges I would likely face in a real world situation. I also learned how to use GitHub and learned about how to troubleshoot problems and communicate with fellow devs. So I'd definitely say I feel more confident!
